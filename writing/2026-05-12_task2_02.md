# IELTS Writing Task 2 — 02
> 날짜: 2026-05-12 | 완료: 2026-05-15 | 최종 Band: 5.5 예상

## 문제

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programmes. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

---

## 모범 답안

Whether unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programmes is widely debated. I strongly believe that making it mandatory allows students to gain diverse experiences and develop valuable life skills that go beyond what the classroom can offer.

First, few students choose to volunteer on their own initiative. When given free time, most teenagers prefer leisure activities over unpaid community service. As a result, many miss the opportunity to experience the joy of helping others. If community service is made compulsory, however, all students would be given the chance to volunteer, regardless of their personal motivation or background.

Secondly, community service provides experiences that formal education simply cannot replicate. By volunteering, students are exposed to people and environments outside their usual social circles, which helps them develop empathy and a broader worldview. For example, cleaning a public park raises awareness of environmental issues, while volunteering at a police station allows students to recognise the extent of social problems in their community. Such exposure can shape their values and sense of responsibility for years to come.

In conclusion, I strongly believe that compulsory community service in high school provides students with valuable experiences and life skills that go beyond what the classroom can offer. The benefits of broadening students' horizons and fostering social awareness far outweigh the concerns about mandatory participation.
