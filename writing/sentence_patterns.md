# Sentence Patterns

## Pattern 1: [명사 + who절], which can V

**구조**
```
[Noun] who [relative clause] may perceive [A] as [B], which can motivate them to [V].
```

**원문 (06번 모범 답안)**
> Offenders who serve longer sentences may perceive imprisonment as a genuine punishment, which can motivate them not to reoffend upon release.

**연습 문장 (2026-06-22)**
> Students who attend online classes may perceive their classmates as online partners, which can weaken social bonds among students.

> Individuals who frequently use social media may perceive their lives as a product, which can motivate them to record every aspect of their daily lives.

## Pattern 2: Research suggests that ... more likely to V

**구조**
```
Research on [X] suggests that individuals with [Y] are significantly more likely to [V] than those without such issues.
```

**원문 (06번 모범 답안)**
> Research on recidivism suggests that individuals with social or psychological problems are significantly more likely to reoffend after release than those without such issues.

**연습 문장 (2026-06-22)**
> Research on smartphone screen time suggests that individuals with addictive behaviours are significantly more likely to struggle to concentrate than those without such issues.

> Research on social media suggests that individuals with addiction are significantly more likely to struggle to concentrate than those without such issues.
