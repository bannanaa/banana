## 문제

Some people think that the best way to improve public health is to increase the number of sports facilities. Others, however, believe that this would have little effect on public health and that other measures are required.

Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

---

## 모범 답안

Whether expanding sports facilities is the most effective way to promote public health has become a subject of considerable debate. While advocates of this approach contend that such facilities broaden access to exercise, others argue that alternative measures would yield more substantial results. In my view, sports facilities alone are insufficient to bring about meaningful improvements in public health.

On the one hand, proponents of sports facilities argue that outdoor equipment in public parks lowers the barrier to physical activity by offering free and accessible opportunities for people of all ages. To illustrate, individuals who cannot afford gym memberships may benefit from such facilities, making exercise more inclusive across different income groups.

On the other hand, there are significant drawbacks to relying solely on this approach. Many people are unfamiliar with how to use the equipment correctly, and exercising in an open public space can feel uncomfortable, leading to passive rather than active participation. I would argue that structured community programmes, such as affordable group fitness classes, are a far more effective solution. When individuals engage in physical activity alongside peers and receive guidance from a qualified instructor, they tend to participate more consistently, which is crucial for achieving genuine, long-term health benefits.

In conclusion, while sports facilities are a valuable addition to public spaces, they are insufficient on their own. Investing in community-based fitness programmes would do considerably more to enhance public health in a lasting way.
