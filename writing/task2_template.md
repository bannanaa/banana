# IELTS Writing Task 2 — 4단락 템플릿
> "Discuss both views + give opinion" 유형 기준

---

## 구조 한눈에 보기

```
Intro      (2~3문장, 약 50단어)
Body 1     (3~4문장, 약 70단어) — View A (한쪽 주장)
Body 2     (3~4문장, 약 80단어) — View B + 내 의견
Conclusion (2문장, 약 50단어)
총 250~280단어
```

---

## 1. INTRO 패턴

```
Whether [주제] is [주장] is a widely debated topic.
While some argue that [View A 한 줄],
others believe that [View B 한 줄].
In my opinion, [내 입장 명확하게].
```

**오늘 문제 예시:**
> Whether increasing sports facilities is the best way to improve public health is a widely debated topic.
> While some argue that expanding such facilities provides accessible opportunities for exercise,
> others believe that alternative measures would be more effective.
> In my opinion, sports facilities alone are insufficient, and structured programmes would yield better results.

**채울 때 규칙:**
- `Whether` + 주제 + `is` + 형용사/명사구 → `is a widely debated topic`
- View A는 찬성 측, View B는 반대 측 한 줄씩
- 내 의견은 마지막에 딱 한 문장으로

---

## 2. BODY 1 패턴 (찬성 측 주장)

```
On the one hand, proponents of [View A] argue that [이유].
[구체적 예시나 설명].
[추가 설명 or 결과].
```

**연결어:** `On the one hand` / `Those who support ~ argue that` / `Advocates of ~ claim that`

**오늘 문제 예시:**
> On the one hand, proponents of sports facilities argue that they provide free and accessible opportunities for exercise.
> Outdoor equipment in public parks, for example, can be used by people of all ages without any membership fees.
> In this sense, they lower the barrier to physical activity and may encourage more people to exercise regularly.

---

## 3. BODY 2 패턴 (반대 측 + 내 의견)

```
On the other hand, there are [significant/clear] drawbacks to [View A].
[한계점 설명].
[추가 한계 or 반례].
I believe that [내 의견 + 이유].
[내 의견 보강].
```

**연결어:** `On the other hand` / `However` / `Nevertheless` / `In contrast`

**오늘 문제 예시:**
> On the other hand, there are significant drawbacks to relying solely on sports facilities.
> Many people are unsure how to use the equipment correctly, which reduces its effectiveness.
> Furthermore, exercising alone in public can feel uncomfortable, leading to passive rather than active participation.
> I believe that structured group classes are a more effective solution, as they provide both guidance and motivation.
> The social environment encourages consistent participation, which is essential for genuine health improvements.

---

## 4. CONCLUSION 패턴

```
In conclusion, while [View A의 장점 인정],
[내 입장 재확인 — intro랑 다른 표현으로].
[한 줄 마무리 제안 or 전망].
```

**오늘 문제 예시:**
> In conclusion, while sports facilities are a useful addition to public spaces, they are insufficient on their own.
> Investing in community-based fitness programmes would do more to improve public health in a lasting way.

---

## 자주 쓰는 연결어 모음

| 역할 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| 찬성 도입 | On the one hand / Those who support ~ argue that |
| 반대 도입 | On the other hand / However / In contrast |
| 예시 | For example / For instance / such as |
| 추가 | Furthermore / Moreover / In addition |
| 결과 | As a result / Therefore / Consequently |
| 결론 | In conclusion / To conclude / Overall |

---

## 고득점 표현 패턴 (외워두면 어디서든 활용)

| 상황 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| ~의 진입장벽을 낮추다 | lower the barrier to ~ |
| 꾸준히 참여하다 | participate consistently |
| 지속적인 방식으로 | in a lasting way |
| 상당한 단점이 있다 | there are significant drawbacks to ~ |
| ~에만 의존하다 | rely solely on ~ |
| ~을 무시할 수 없다 | cannot be overlooked |
| 더 나은 결과를 내다 | yield better results |
| ~을 가능하게 하다 | enable / facilitate |
