> IELTS Writing 학습 중 수집한 단어/표현

## 2026-05-08 (Task2 01 — Sports facilities & public health)

| 단어/표현 | 뜻 | 예문 |
|----------|-----|------|
| `advocate` (n) | 지지자, 옹호자 | *Advocates of this approach contend that it is effective.* |
| `contend` (v) | 주장하다 | *Proponents contend that sports facilities lower the barrier to exercise.* |
| `proponent` (n) | 지지자, 찬성론자 | *Proponents of sports facilities argue that they are accessible.* |
| `drawback` (n) | 단점, 결점 | *There are significant drawbacks to relying solely on this approach.* |
| `solely` (adv) | 오로지, ~만 | *We should not rely solely on sports facilities.* |
| `leading to passive` (표현) | 수동적인 참여로 이어지는 | *...leading to passive rather than active participation.* ← 통째로 외우기 |
| `affordable` (adj) | 저렴한, 부담 없는 | *Affordable group fitness classes are a more effective solution.* |
| `alongside` (prep) | ~와 함께, ~옆에서 | *When people exercise alongside peers, they stay more motivated.* |
| `invest in` (표현) | ~에 투자하다 | *Investing in community-based programmes yields lasting results.* |

---

### 헷갈리기 쉬운 것
- `advocate` vs `proponent` — 둘 다 "지지자"지만 번갈아 쓰면 어휘 다양성 점수 ↑
- `contend` vs `argue` — 같은 뜻, 번갈아 쓸 것
- `solely` = only 의 격식체 버전 → IELTS에서 `only` 대신 쓰면 좋음
- `affordable` = cheap의 격식체 → `cheap` 대신 항상 이걸로
